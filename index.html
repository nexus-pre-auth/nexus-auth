The Strategy

From Clinics to Major Payers

The business model is evolutionary. We begin by solving pain points for small clinics. As the "Data Watcher" ingests millions of rules and outcomes, it builds the industry's most accurate Logic Map.

  • 1
    Target Small Clinics

    High pain, low barrier to entry. Rapid deployment of Data Watcher.

  • 2
    Aggregate Logic

    Machine learning identifies hidden approval patterns across payers.

  • 3
    Sell "Clean Claims"

    Sell the data/insights to Major Payers to reduce their own processing costs.

The Value Crossover

As clinic adoption grows, the strategic value of the Logic Map surpasses the software revenue.

The Product

Monetizing "Clean Claim" Insights

By aggregating outcomes, NexusAuth creates a heat map of payer behaviors. We sell this transparency back to payers and large provider networks, showing them exactly where and why claims succeed or fail.

Pre-Emptive Validation

The system flags a claim *before* submission if it falls into a "Red Zone" (low probability) on the map, prompting specific corrections based on historical successes.

Payer Rule Reverse-Engineering

Even if payers don't publish their rules, the Logic Map deduces them through statistical analysis of thousands of transaction outcomes.
